OUR JOURNEY

Learning to code by yourself is a very ambitious and tedious journey. You will be faced with lots of self-doubt along the way and you will feel lost multiple times. Nonetheless, we are here to help you and guid you every step of they way.

We know what it's like to be a self-taught programmer and have faced multiple adversities along the journey. With tons of information online or on YouTube videos, and the countless coding courses online, you will feel overwhelmed and stuck. Our goal is to illuminate the most prominent route for each of our clients. Not every coding language is the same and neither every self-taught journey.

Our mission is to make sure you reach your programming skills as proficient as possible and guide you throughout. We also offer constructive feedback from your portfolio website to your portfolio projects.

OUR HISTORY

Our company's origins trace back to one self-taught programmer who, having navigated the challenging path of learning coding independently, recognized the need for better resources and guidance amidst the overwhelming abundance of information and technologies available.

We aimed to offer adequate resources and mentorshiptailored to the unique needs of each learner. Over time,our commitment to this mission has borne fruit, as we've helped countless subscribers break into the tech industry in various roles, from HTML email developers and web developers to backend developers, full-stack developers, and software engineers.

Our success lies not only in the breadth of opportunities we've opened but also in the confidence and skills we've instilled in our community members, empowering them to thrive in the ever-evolving landscape of technology.
