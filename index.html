SELFDEV JOURNEY

Learning to code by yourself is a very ambitious and tedious journey. You will be faced with lots of self-doubt along the way and you will feel lost multiple times. Nonetheless, we are here to help you and guid you every step of they way.

We know what it's like to be a self-taught programmer and have faced multiple adversities along the journey. With tons of information online or on YouTube videos, and the countless coding courses online, you will feel overwhelmed and stuck. Our goal is to illuminate the most prominent route for each of our clients. Not every coding language is the same and neither every self-taught journey.

Our mission is to make sure you reach your programming skills as proficient as possible and guide you throughout. We also offer constructive feedback from your portfolio website to your portfolio projects.

Feedback Mentorship

The self-taught programming course I am describing is designed to provide ample feedback and mentorship to ensure successful learning of coding skills. This is important because programming can be challenging, and having guidance from experienced mentors can prevent frustration and help learners overcome obstacles. With regular check-ins, personalized guidance, and constructive feedback, students can receive valuable insights, stay motivated, and improve their coding abilities.

Mentorship can also provide invaluable networking opportunities and real-world insights, enhancing the learning experience and improving job prospects in the field.
